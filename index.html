ABOUT PARIKSA

Bridging the Gap for Urban Resilience

Bandung City faces complex geological and hydrometeorological threats, from the active Lembang Fault to significant land subsidence and extreme weather flooding. Currently, existing disaster risk maps are restricted to a macro scale, limiting effective response and situational awareness. Team Pariksa is developing a High-Precision Disaster Risk Map at a 1:5,000 scale to identify these multi-hazard risks down to the specific property or parcel level. We transform complex geospatial data into actionable spatial intelligence, providing the government, urban planners, and communities with the certainty needed to protect critical assets and save lives.

STRATEGIC CAPABILITIES

Empowering Data-Driven Mitigation

We provide robust spatial analysis and comprehensive risk calculations to optimize your disaster preparedness protocols.

Parcel-Level Precision

Unlike conventional macro-scale maps, Pariksa identifies potential hazards down to the smallest spatial unit: the land parcel. Our 1:5,000 scale mapping allows stakeholders to evaluate specific vulnerabilities for individual buildings and critical infrastructure with maximum accuracy.

Multi-Hazard Integration

We synthesize overlapping threat layers—including Lembang Fault seismic activity, historical flood zones, and landslide susceptibility—into a single, unified WebGIS interface. This comprehensive approach enables a holistic evaluation of urban risks in Bandung City.

Scientific Risk Algorithm

Our platform calculates standardized risk scores utilizing the proven R = H × (V/C) methodology. By integrating Hazard potential, socio-physical Vulnerability, and regional Capacity, we deliver evidence-based recommendations for targeted mitigation.

Economic & Social Resilience

Proactive mitigation is a highly efficient investment. Our financial analysis demonstrates a Benefit-Cost Ratio of 18,161.42, indicating that every single rupiah invested in Pariksa's precision mapping can potentially save thousands in future disaster impacts.
